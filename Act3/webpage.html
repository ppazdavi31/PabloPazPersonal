A cat café (猫カフェ?) is a public establishment where customers can play with or observe cats. It typically serves coffee or tea, and some have a reading area. Customers pay an entrance fee, usually for a specific time period. Wiki

If your love for cats is so great that you could even eat at the same table with them, this place is for you. It's called Catfecito in the Condesa neighborhood. At this establishment, felines are the absolute stars. But before you're literally by their side, you should know the rules: no feeding them human food, no pulling their tails or ears, and no bothering them, among others. Upon entering, you'll see a small boutique on the right where you'll find all kinds of cat-themed jewelry: earrings, necklaces, rings, as well as mugs and keychains. There are also pictures of cats for sale on the walls.
